About the App

Spellathon is a popular word game developed to educate young children and adults.
In this game, 7 alphabets are arranged in a hexagon. Players must make words of 4 or more alphabets.
Each word must contain the center alphabet and there must be at least one 7 letter word.
This word game helps children and adults in increasing their English vocabulary.
