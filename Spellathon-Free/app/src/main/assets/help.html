Getting Started

Thankyou for downloading this app. To get started, follow the below simple steps:

How to Play:

To start playing, select a random game or continue with your last paused game.
To know the rules of game, see the example.

Rules of Game:

Random alphabets are arranged in a hexagon. Make words of 4 or more alphabets. Each word must contain the center alphabet. Each alphabet can be used only once. There must be at least one 7 letter word. Plurals, foreign words and proper names are not allowed.
English dictionary is taken as a reference.
